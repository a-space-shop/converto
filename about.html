PDF tools that just work.

EditPDF.fyi is a free suite of online PDF tools — no account required, no watermarks, no limits. Built for people who just need to get something done.

Why we built this

Working with PDFs should not require an expensive subscription or a 500MB desktop application. Most people need to do a handful of things: convert a PDF to Word so they can edit it, merge a few files before sending them, compress a large scan to get it under an email attachment limit, or convert a Word document to PDF before submitting it. These are simple tasks that should take ten seconds.

EditPDF was built to make those tasks instant and free. The tools that can run entirely in your browser do exactly that — compress, merge, split, and edit all process locally on your device so your files never leave your computer. The tools that need server processing — conversions between PDF, Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and image formats — use LibreOffice and carefully tested Python libraries on our servers, with every uploaded file automatically and permanently deleted within 60 minutes.

There is no premium tier, no usage cap, and no sign-up. The site is kept free through advertising and optional donations from users who find the tools useful.
