1. Files You Upload

EditPDF offers two types of tools:

  • Browser-based tools (Compress PDF, Merge PDF, Split PDF, Edit PDF): These tools process your file entirely within your own web browser using JavaScript. Your file is never transmitted to our servers and never leaves your device. We have no access to these files at any time.
  • Server-side conversion tools (PDF to Word, Word to PDF, PDF to Excel, JPG to PDF, Convert PDF): These tools upload your file to our servers over an encrypted HTTPS connection for processing. Files are processed using LibreOffice and Python libraries, then immediately queued for deletion. Every uploaded file is automatically and permanently deleted within 60 minutes. We do not read, index, or share the contents of uploaded files.

2. Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by websites you visit. We use cookies for the following purposes:

Google Analytics (_ga, _gid)Measures site traffic and usage patterns anonymouslyFirst-party analytics

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some features on this site.

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on this website. Google AdSense is operated by Google LLC, 1600 Amphitheatre Parkway, Mountain View, CA 94043, USA.

Google AdSense uses cookies, including the DoubleClick DART cookie, to serve ads based on your visits to this site and other sites on the internet. Personalised advertising means Google may use your browsing history to show you ads relevant to your interests.

You can opt out of personalised advertising in the following ways:

4. Analytics — Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics (GA4) to understand how visitors use this site. Google Analytics collects anonymised data such as pages visited, time spent on pages, and general geographic region. This data is used solely to improve the website.

Google Analytics uses first-party cookies (_ga, _gid) to identify returning visitors. IP addresses are anonymised. We do not use Google Analytics to collect personally identifiable information.

You can prevent your data from being collected by Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

5. What Personal Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Usage data: Pages visited, time on site, browser type, operating system, and general geographic region — collected anonymously via Google Analytics
  • Advertising data: Browsing behaviour used by Google AdSense to serve relevant advertisements (managed by Google, not stored by us)
  • Contact data: If you contact us by email, we retain your email address and the content of your message solely to respond to your query

We do not collect your name, physical address, payment information, or any account credentials. We do not require you to create an account to use any tool on this site.

6. How We Share Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. Data may be shared in the following limited circumstances:

  • Google: Usage data via Google Analytics and advertising data via Google AdSense, as described above
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information where required to do so by law or in response to a valid legal request
  • Business transfer: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of the business, user data may be transferred as part of that transaction

7. Data Retention

Uploaded files are automatically and permanently deleted within 60 minutes of upload. Browser-based tool files are never stored — they exist only in your browser's memory while the tab is open.

Google Analytics data is retained for 14 months in accordance with Google's standard retention policy. Email correspondence is retained for as long as necessary to resolve your query.

8. Security

All file transfers to and from our servers use HTTPS encryption. Our servers are hosted with industry-standard security practices. No method of electronic transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
