How to convert PDF to Word

Converting a PDF to Word with EditPDF takes just a few seconds. Upload your PDF file by dragging it into the box above or clicking to browse your files. Once uploaded, click the Convert to Word button. Our servers process your file using LibreOffice and advanced Python libraries, then produce a fully editable DOCX file you can download immediately. The whole process typically completes in under 30 seconds even for multi-page documents.

PDF to Word conversion is one of the most commonly needed document tasks. PDFs are designed for viewing and sharing with consistent formatting across any device, but they lock your content in place. Word documents, on the other hand, are designed for editing. When you receive a contract, a report, or a form as a PDF and need to update the text, change figures, or reformat sections, converting it to Word is the fastest route.

Our converter preserves the elements that matter most in professional documents: text formatting including bold, italic and underline, paragraph spacing, fonts where they are available on the system, tables with their row and column structure, and embedded images. For straightforward business documents, the output is typically clean enough to use immediately with only minor touch-ups if any.

Privacy is built into how EditPDF works. Your PDF is uploaded over an encrypted HTTPS connection, processed on our secure servers, and permanently and automatically deleted within 60 minutes. We never read the content of your documents, never store copies, and never share files with third parties.

Frequently asked questions

Is the PDF to Word conversion accurate?
We use LibreOffice combined with advanced Python libraries to preserve text, fonts, tables, images, and layout as closely as possible. Most standard business documents convert with high accuracy. Complex PDFs with unusual multi-column layouts or custom embedded fonts may have minor differences in appearance, but all text content will be fully extracted and editable.
Is my file safe when I upload it?
Yes. Files are uploaded over an encrypted HTTPS connection, processed on our secure servers, and permanently deleted within 60 minutes. We never read, store, or share your documents.
What is the maximum file size?
The maximum file size for PDF to Word conversion is 25MB. If your file is larger, use our Compress PDF tool first to reduce the file size, then convert the compressed version.
Can I convert a scanned PDF to Word?
Scanned PDFs contain images of text rather than actual text characters, which makes direct conversion significantly more challenging. Our converter works best with digitally created PDFs where the text is real, selectable content.
What word processors can open the converted DOCX file?
The output is a standard DOCX file compatible with Microsoft Word, Google Docs, LibreOffice Writer, Apple Pages, and WPS Office.
Do I need to create an account to use the converter?
No account is required. The PDF to Word converter is completely free with no sign-up, no subscription, and no usage limits.
How long does the conversion take?
Most PDF to Word conversions complete in under 30 seconds. Larger documents may take a little longer. The progress bar updates in real time so you can track the conversion as it happens.
