Free PDF tools for every task

EditPDF is a free online suite of PDF tools covering everything you need to work with PDF files day to day. Convert PDFs to Word, Excel and PowerPoint. Convert Word documents and images to PDF. Merge multiple PDFs into one, split a single PDF into separate files, compress large PDFs to a fraction of their original size, and annotate or fill PDFs directly in your browser. Every tool is available immediately — no account, no subscription, no software to install.

For conversions that require server processing — PDF to Word, Word to PDF, PDF to Excel, and image to PDF — your files are uploaded over an encrypted HTTPS connection, processed using LibreOffice and best-in-class Python libraries, and permanently deleted from our servers within 60 minutes. We never read the content of your documents and never share your files with any third party. For tools that run entirely in your browser — compress, merge, split and edit — your file never leaves your device, making those operations completely private by design.
