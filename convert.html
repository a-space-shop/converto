Frequently Asked Questions

What file formats can I convert?
The converter supports PDF, Word (DOCX and DOC), Excel (XLSX), PowerPoint (PPTX), JPG, PNG, HTML, and several other formats. Select your source format and target format from the dropdowns to see available conversion paths.
How accurate is the conversion?
We use LibreOffice and best-in-class Python libraries to preserve text, fonts, tables, images, and layout through conversion. For most standard business documents the output is highly accurate and requires no cleanup.
Is there a file size limit?
The maximum file size is 25MB. Most documents, spreadsheets, and presentations are well within this limit. If your file is larger, try compressing it first.
How long does conversion take?
Most conversions complete in under 10 seconds. Larger files may take a little longer. The progress bar updates in real time during conversion.
Are my files kept private?
Yes. Files are uploaded over an encrypted connection, processed on secure servers, and permanently deleted within 60 minutes. We do not read your document content or retain any copy of your files after conversion.
Do I need to sign up?
No. All conversions are completely free with no account required. Upload your file, convert it, and download the result. There are no usage limits and no hidden fees.
What is the difference between this tool and the dedicated converters?
This universal converter handles many format combinations in one place. The dedicated tools such as PDF to Word, Word to PDF, JPG to PDF, and PDF to Excel are optimised specifically for their conversion type and may offer a slightly more focused experience. Both use the same underlying conversion engine and produce the same quality output.
